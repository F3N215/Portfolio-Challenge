Refactoring Challenge

 

This project was a prime example of working with existing code to refactor and update an existing page to match accessibility criteria. Working with Horieson, a full-service digital marketing agency, I was tasked with assessing and updating code on Horieson's website assigned by my tech lead. By working to improve the HTML code without changing how it functions, and keeping the overall page within 90% of its likeness, I worked to ensure that Horieson's website adheres to accessibility criteria to ensure people with disabilities can still capably use the site.

Meet the Major Arcana

 

A personal work-in-progress project, I'm working to create a functioning database of Tarot cards designed to introduce new and curious individuals to all cards belonging to the major arcana and to explain their meaning. In Tarot, there are twenty-two iconic major arcana (also known as trump cards), and fifty-six belonging to the minor arcana made up of suits: cups, coins, wands & swords. Major arcana cards represent various forces, characters, virtues and vices.
